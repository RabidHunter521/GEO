# SeenBy — Feature Overview

SeenBy is an AI visibility tracking platform for agencies. It tells brands whether
AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude are recommending them
— and what to do about it if they're not.

---

## Core Feature

**AI Visibility Scanning** — On demand, SeenBy asks real questions to ChatGPT,
Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude (the platforms people actually use to research
brands today) and checks whether the client's brand shows up in the answer.
This is the foundation everything else is built on.

- 8 real-world questions per scan, covering 4 categories:
  - Brand searches (do people find them when searching their own name?)
  - Comparisons (brand vs. competitors)
  - Recommendations ("what's the best X in [industry]?")
  - Local searches ("best X in Kuala Lumpur / Malaysia")
- Runs across up to 4 AI platforms per client, each toggleable per client
- If one platform is temporarily unavailable, the scan still completes using
  the rest — nothing blocks the client's results
- Every scan is logged with full history for trend tracking

---

## GEO Score (0–100)

A single score that tells a client, at a glance, how visible they are to AI.

- Combines 5 weighted dimensions:
  - **AI Citability (40%)** — automatic, from the scan engine
  - **Brand Authority (20%)** — assessed by the SeenBy team
  - **Content Quality (20%)** — assessed by the SeenBy team
  - **Technical Foundations (10%)** — automatic, verified site setup
  - **Structured Data (10%)** — automatic, verified site setup
- Scored against clear bands: Excellent, Good, Fair, Developing, Low
- Tracked over time so clients can see progress month over month

---

## Competitor Intelligence

- Tracks up to 5 competitors per client
- Shows how the client's visibility compares to competitors across the same
  AI platforms and query categories
- Trend charts over recent scans, so agencies can show "we're closing the gap"
- Competitor overtake alerts — flags the moment a competitor pulls ahead

---

## Win/Loss Query Analysis

- Breaks down every scan question into a win, loss, or open opportunity
- Shows exactly which questions the client is winning, which ones a
  competitor is winning instead, and where there's no clear winner yet
- Gives agencies a concrete, query-level conversation to have with clients

---

## 90-Day Content Roadmap

- Turns the queries a client is *losing* to competitors into a prioritized,
  time-phased content plan
- Generated by Claude AI — practical content ideas the client's team (or the
  agency) can act on over the next 90 days
- Directly tied to real competitive gaps, not generic content advice

---

## Content Gap Analysis & Content Briefs

- Identifies topic and entity gaps in the client's existing content
- On-demand, AI-generated content briefs for specific queries the client is
  losing — ready-to-use direction for writers

---

## AI Readiness Toolkit

Automatically generates the technical files AI crawlers look for:

- **llms.txt** — following the emerging AI content standard
- **schema.json** — structured data (LocalBusiness, Organization, FAQ) so AI
  systems understand who the business is
- **robots.txt** — explicitly allows AI crawlers (ChatGPT, Perplexity,
  Claude, Google AI) to access the site

Each file comes with copy/download buttons and plain-English setup
instructions. A built-in verification check confirms the files are live on
the client's site, and automatically updates the Technical Foundations and
Structured Data scores once verified.

---

## GEO Action Center

- After every scan, Claude AI generates 3–5 prioritized action items
- Each action shows an estimated score impact, calculated using a consistent,
  auditable formula — not just an AI guess
- Gives clients (and agencies) a clear "what to do next" list instead of raw
  data

---

## Industry Benchmarking

- Anonymously compares a client's score against other SeenBy clients in the
  same industry
- Shows percentile ranking — "you're ahead of X% of similar businesses"
- Only shown when there's enough data in a category to keep individual
  competitors' numbers private

---

## Reporting

**Weekly Digest (automated email)**
- Sent automatically with the client's current visibility score
- Includes an AI-generated tip or recommended action when the score moves by
  5+ points

**Monthly PDF Report**
- Full visibility report generated automatically, reviewed by the SeenBy team
  before sending
- Includes a Claude-written "What Changed This Month" narrative — a
  plain-English summary of what moved and why
- Delivered by email; also viewable in the client's read-only portal

---

## Alerts (Admin)

Automatic alerts the moment something needs attention:
- Score drops
- A competitor overtakes the client
- Unusual or inaccurate AI responses ("hallucinations") about the client

Alerts go to email, and optionally to Telegram for instant notification —
without ever blocking or slowing down a scan.

---

## Agency Admin Dashboard

- Portfolio view of all clients at a glance: scores, recent changes, and a
  "needs attention" queue
- Filter and segment clients by score, industry, or location
- Bulk actions — trigger scans across multiple clients at once
- Full activity log per client for transparency and audit history

---

## Client View (Read-Only Portal)

- Each client gets a secure, private link (no login required) to view their
  own results
- Includes their visibility score, scan results, competitor comparison, and
  delivered reports
- Read-only and scoped — clients only ever see their own data, in
  client-friendly language (no technical jargon, no raw AI data)

---

## Why It Matters

Search behavior is shifting from Google to AI assistants. SeenBy gives
agencies a repeatable way to measure, report on, and improve how visible
their clients are inside that shift — with concrete actions, not just a
score.
